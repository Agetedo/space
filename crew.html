LAUNCH VEHICLE

A launch vehicle or carrier rocket is a rocket- propelled vehicle used to carry a payload from Earth's surface to space, usually to Earth orbit or beyond. Our WEB-X carrier rocket is the most powerful in opeation. Standingh 150 metres tall, it's quite an awe-inspiring sight on the launch pad!

Primary cosmic rays

Particles of cosmic rays coming from outside the earth's atmosphere (as opposed to secondary cosmic rays, the particles of which are formed during collisions of primary cosmic rays with molecules of gases in the Earth's atmosphere).

Solar system

A system that includes the Sun and the planets revolving around it with their satellites, minor planets, comets, meteoroids and other objects moving around the Sun.
